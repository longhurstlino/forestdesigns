 About us

Since 1995, Forest Designs have been based in Sussex, specialising in cabinet making and joinery.
With an eye for detail and quality we create high spec, bespoke work, providing a personal service to ensure that your requirements are met.
At our workshop in Ditchling, we design and manufacture your furniture, finishing to a high standard before installation.
